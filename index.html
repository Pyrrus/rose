Petal of the Rose

The name of the game is Petals Around the Rose. The name of the game is important. The computer will roll one die and ask you to guess the score for the roll. Your mission is to work out how the computer calculates the score and become a Potentate of the Rose.

Hint:

  • You need to think of a rose.
